Spaghetti Carbonara

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the 200g pancetta, having first removed any rind.
  3. Finely grate both cheeses and mix them together.
  4. Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl, season with a little freshly grated black pepper and set everything aside.
  5. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti and cook according to the pack instructions.
  6. Squash 2 peeled plump garlic cloves with the blade of a knife.
  7. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the pancetta with the garlic.
  8. Keep the heat hot but not too hot to avoid burning the garlic.
  9. Remove the garlic from the pan when it turns brown and discard.
  10. Keep on frying the pancetta until it's golden and crisp.
  11. Keep everything warm while the pasta finishes cooking.
  12. Reserve a cup of the cooking water and drain the spaghetti.
  13. Add the spaghetti to the pancetta and stir to coat in the fat.
  14. Take the pan off the heat and quickly pour in the eggs and cheese.
  15. Use tongs or a long fork to lift up the spaghetti so it mixes easily with the egg mixture.
  16. Add extra pasta cooking water to keep it saucy.
  17. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese.
