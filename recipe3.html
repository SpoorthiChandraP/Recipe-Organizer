Caesar Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 anchovy fillets
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 head Romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons

Instructions

  1. Mash the garlic and anchovy fillets into a paste.
  2. Whisk in the egg yolks and lemon juice until well combined.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking constantly to emulsify.
  4. Stir in the Parmesan cheese.
  5. Toss the Romaine lettuce with the dressing until evenly coated.
  6. Top with croutons and additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
